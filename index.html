Our Mission

Ever since Industrial Revolution, humans have been slowly destroying the planet. With pollution from cars and factories and the billions of pounds of plastic dumped into the ocean every year, it is essential that humanity finds a way to stop damaging the environment. This website was made to raise awareness about this problem, and to also help individuals understand the effect they themselves have on the planet. When people see the direct impact they've made to the enviroment, it will help them understand the actions that need to be taken to save Earth.
