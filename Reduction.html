Carbon Footprint Reduction

There are numerous ways you can shrink your carbon footprint, here are 10:

  • Choose organic and local foods
  • Reduce your food waste
  • Wash your clothing in cold water
  • Buy less stuff and buy used or recycled items whenever possible
  • Bring your own reusable bag when you shop
  • Switch lights off when you leave a room
  • Change incandescent light bulbs to LEDs
  • Drive less
  • Consider purchasing a hybrid or electric vehicle
  • Avoid flying on an airplane if possible
